Experience Overview

This 7-day sailing trip follows a defined route along the Amalfi Coast. The itinerary includes six nights of on-board accommodation in shared or private cabins on a professional sailing vessel.

A professional crew manages all navigation and boat operations. Daily inclusions consist of breakfast and light lunches prepared on board. The route covers key coastal locations including Sorrento, Capri, and Positano, with scheduled stops for swimming and shore visits.

Trip Itinerary

1

Arrival in Sorrento

Check-in at the Sorrento marina. Crew briefing and cabin assignment followed by an evening in the town center.

2

Capri Transit

Sail to Capri with anchoring near Faraglioni. Scheduled time for swimming and shore access via dinghy.

3

Positano Stop

Transit to Positano. The afternoon is allocated for independent exploration of the village and beaches.

4

Amalfi & Ravello

Docking in Amalfi. Ground transport available for the route to Ravello for viewpoints and garden visits.

Destination Highlights

Accessible Sea Caves

The itinerary includes stops at limestone sea caves. Access is dependent on tide levels and weather conditions.

Hillside Path Network

Ravello is accessed via a shuttle or uphill walking paths from Amalfi, providing elevated views of the coastline.

FAQ & Policies

What is the cancellation policy? add

Full refunds are available for cancellations made at least 30 days prior to departure. Within 30 days, credit is issued for future bookings.

Is sailing experience required? add

No experience is required. The vessel is operated by a professional skipper. Passengers may participate in boat handling if they choose.

What is the recommended travel window? add

The season runs from May through September. Weather conditions are most stable during the mid-summer months.
