Booking Process

How to book a tour? expand_more

Simply browse our destinations, select your preferred date, and follow the guided steps to secure your spot. We require basic traveler information and payment details to finalize your request.

When will I receive my confirmation? expand_more

Most bookings are confirmed instantly. You will receive an email confirmation and a digital receipt within 15 minutes of a successful transaction.

Schedules & Changes

Can I manage my trip dates? expand_more

Yes, you can modify trip dates through your account dashboard. Availability varies by tour operator, and some changes may incur a small adjustment fee.

How do notification settings work? expand_more

We send critical updates via email and SMS. You can customize these preferences in your profile settings to receive only the information you need.

Payments

Which payment methods are secure? expand_more

We accept all major credit cards and verified digital wallets. All transactions are encrypted and processed through industry-standard secure gateways.

How do I access my receipts? expand_more

Receipts are stored indefinitely in your TravelBook account. Navigate to 'My Bookings' and select the specific trip to download your PDF invoice.

Refunds

Am I eligible for a refund? expand_more

Eligibility depends on the cancellation policy of the specific tour, which is displayed at the time of booking. Generally, cancellations made 48 hours in advance are eligible for full refunds.

What is the refund timeline? expand_more

Once approved, refunds typically appear in your bank account within 5 to 10 business days, depending on your financial institution's processing times.
