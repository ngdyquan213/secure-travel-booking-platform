Experience Overview

Set sail on a curated maritime adventure along Italy's most storied coastline. This seven-day voyage balances the rhythmic calm of the Tyrrhenian Sea with the vibrant energy of vertical cliffside villages.

Our professional crew handles the navigation while you immerse yourself in the art of Mediterranean living. From the lemon groves of Sorrento to the hidden grottos only accessible by water, every moment is designed to be intentional, scenic, and deeply refreshing.

Trip Itinerary

1

Arrival in Sorrento

Meet your crew at the marina for a welcome cocktail. Spend the evening exploring Sorrento's historic center before settling into your cabin.

2

The Shores of Capri

Anchor off the Faraglioni rocks. Enjoy a day of swimming in crystal clear waters and an optional guided walk to the Piazzetta.

3

Positano Panorama

Sail towards the iconic vertical village. Afternoon at leisure for boutique shopping or beach relaxation at Spiaggia Grande.

4

Amalfi & Ravello

Explore the maritime history of Amalfi and take a shuttle up to Ravello's mountaintop gardens for breathtaking views.

Destination Highlights

Hidden Grottos

Discover sea caves only accessible by small dinghies, where light plays magical tricks on the water.

Hillside Gardens

Walk through the historic villas of Ravello, offering the highest and most tranquil views of the coast.

May to September offers the best sailing weather. July and August are the peak season and most vibrant.
